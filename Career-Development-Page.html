About Us

The Career Development Wing operates under the Academics and Career Council of IIT Kanpur, dedicated to enhancing students' professional growth.

Our Mission

The Career Development Wing aims to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements by providing comprehensive training, resources, and opportunities to students. We strive to equip every student with the skills and confidence needed to excel in their professional careers.

Our Vision

To create a robust ecosystem that nurtures professional growth, facilitates industry connections, and empowers students to achieve their career aspirations, making IIT Kanpur a hub for future leaders and innovators.

Our Services

Career Counseling

One-on-one sessions with experienced counselors to help students identify their strengths and career paths.

Resume Reviews

Detailed feedback on resumes to help students create impactful documents that highlight their achievements.

Mock Interviews

Practice interviews with industry professionals to prepare students for real-world interview scenarios.

Workshops

Regular workshops on various topics like technical skills, soft skills, and industry trends.

Internship Training Program (ITP)

Our flagship program designed to prepare students for securing internships at top companies through comprehensive training and guidance.

Program Overview

The Internship Training Program is a structured initiative that equips students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in internship interviews and placements. The program covers technical preparation, soft skills development, resume building, and mock interviews.

Comprehensive technical preparation for coding interviews

Resume building workshops and personalized feedback

GeeksForGeeks Partnership

The Career Development Wing has established a strategic collaboration with GeeksForGeeks, one of India's leading platforms for computer science education. This partnership provides IIT Kanpur students with exclusive access to premium learning resources, coding competitions, and placement preparation materials.

Free access to GeeksForGeeks Premium courses

Exclusive webinars and workshops by industry experts
